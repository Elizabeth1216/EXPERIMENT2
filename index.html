Experiment of Light Transmittance of Unidirectional Fluoroscopy Film
单向透视膜的透光实验


Control Group 1                        对照组1
Color:Transparent                   颜色：透明
Transmittance:99%                透光率：99%
UV blocking rate:0%               紫外线阻隔率：0%


Experimental Group 2         实验组2
Color:Silver                               颜色：银色
Transmittance:85%                透光率：85%
UV blocking rate:90%            紫外线阻隔率：90%


Experimental Group 3         实验组3
Color:Gray                                颜色：灰色

Experimental Group 4         实验组4

UV blocking rate:99%            紫外线阻隔率：99%

The experiment shows that the one-way perspective film, which is the main material used to make the mask, has been tested. Four different transparent materials were selected for the light experiment and one-way transmission experiment of the coating. In the process of research, we have done the light transmission test of these four kinds of membranes.The darker perspective film also has a better effect of blocking ultraviolet rays.

我们先对我们找到的四种透视膜的透光度和紫外线透过率做了分析。并且发现深色的透视膜还有隔离紫外线的效果。

Experiment of Light
光线实验


The experiment found that the bright side of the light showed more clarity, and the "unidirectional light transmittance" of the unidirectional perspective film was affected by the light.
实验发现，光的亮面更清晰，且单向透视膜的“单向透光率”受到光的影响。 

It is not recommended to use our products in very bright or dim environments.
不建议在强光和昏暗的环境中使用我们的产品。  

Experiment of Coating
镀层实验


It was found that from the side of the coating, the inner object is relatively blurred and has a reflection, similar to the mirror surface.
实验发现，从有涂层的一侧看，内部物体相对模糊，并有反射，效果类似于镜面。

Our products are made of material similar to that of experimental group 2.
我们的产品采用和实验组2类似的材质。
